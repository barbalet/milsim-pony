# Cycle 20 Capture Notes

## Capture Format

- Open the overhead map at district handoffs.
- Raise the 4x scope at skyline-heavy stops.
- Use the pause shell after major district captures when a route-proof shell frame is needed.

## Capture Sequence

1. `Woden Town Centre Staging`
   Capture the route start and confirm the shell/map frame the run as a review pack instead of only a survey route.
2. `Woden Scope Perch`
   Raise the scope and capture the Woden towers against Hindmarsh Drive.
3. `State Circle Transfer`
   Capture the handoff through State Circle, Adelaide Avenue, and Sydney Avenue.
4. `East Basin Lookout`
   Capture the lake edge, Kings Avenue approach, and eastern ridge in one frame.
5. `Constitution Axis Review`
   Capture Constitution Avenue and Kings Avenue as one basin-core read.
6. `Civic Interchange Review`
   Open the map and capture Northbourne, Alinga, and Civic massing without developer narration.
7. `West Basin Promenade Review`
   Capture Commonwealth Avenue west and the shoreline handoff into Yarralumla.
8. `Black Mountain Scope Perch`
   Raise the scope and capture Telstra Tower plus the Bruce saddle.
9. `Belconnen Town Centre Review`
   Capture Belconnen Way, the interchange, and the town centre towers as one lane read.
10. `Ginninderra Drive Review`
   Capture the northern lake edge and Ginninderra Drive exit read as the route finish.

## Shell Proofs

- Capture the title shell once before starting the route.
- Capture the pause shell at least once mid-route after a comparison stop is active.
- Capture the completion shell after `Ginninderra Drive Review`.
